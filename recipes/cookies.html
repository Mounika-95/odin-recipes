Cake Mix Cookies

Description

I love how soft these cookies are and the sprinkles add a fun pop of color! These easy funfetti cookies are perfect for kids, a birthday party, St. Patrick’s Day, any spring holiday, and sweet summertime! We love sprinkles so much we have a fun too! If you love confetti cake, you will love these fun recipes and colorful cookies.

Ingredients

  • 1 box Funfetti cake mix , or white cake mix (you will need to add your own sprinkles to the batter)
  • 2 TBSP sprinkles, optional
  • 1/3 cup canola oil
  • 2 eggs
  • Rainbow Sprinkles, Nonpareils or Jimmies for rolling or sprinkling, optional

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix together the dry cake mix, vegetable oil, and eggs.
  2. Add additional sprinkles to the batter if desired. Mix until the cake batter is moist and all ingredients are blended together well.
  3. Shape the cookie dough into 1 inch balls or use a 1-inch cookie scoop. Roll the dough into extra sprinkles or top with extra sprinkles if you wish. Drop them onto a non-stick baking sheet or pre-greased pan.
  4. Bake the cookies at 350 degrees for 8 – 10 minutes.
  5. Once they are done baking, place the cookies on a cooling rack or parchment paper before enjoying.
